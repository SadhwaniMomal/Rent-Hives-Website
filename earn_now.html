How to Rent a Dress with Rent Hives?

Here are Simple Steps!

Sign Up or Log In

New Users: Create an account on our website or app.

Returning Users: Log in to your existing account.

Browse and Select

Explore: Browse our collection by categories like Wedding, Party, etc.

Filters: Use size, color, and price filters to find the perfect dress.

Details: Click on a dress for more info and to check availability.

Virtual Try-On (Optional)

AR Feature: Virtually try on the dress to see how it looks on you.

Add to Cart

Rental Period: Choose how long you want to rent (e.g., 3 days, 7 days).

Accessories: Add matching jewelry, bags, or shoes if needed.

Checkout

Review Order: Check your order summary and apply any discount codes.

Delivery Info: Enter your address and choose a delivery option.

Payment: Complete your payment using our secure gateway.

Enjoy and Return

Wear: Enjoy your rented dress for your event.

Return: Pack the dress in the return packaging and schedule a pickup or drop it off as instructed. We handle the cleaning.

Here's how we do it: Tackling Waste: By promoting borrowing over buying, we help reduce the amount of waste generated by unnecessary purchases. Instead of adding to the landfill, items are shared and reused, minimizing environmental impact. Empowering Budgets: We understand that not everyone can afford to buy expensive items for occasional use. That's why we offer affordable rental options, giving everyone access to the things they need without breaking the bank. Unique Proposition: Our focus on the borrowing trend comes with a unique twist. We provide a range of quality options for our customers to choose from, ensuring that they can find something that fits their style and budget. Additionally, we go the extra mile by disinfecting every outfit before it's rented out, addressing concerns about hygiene and safety. Diverse Fashion Options: While our primary focus is on borrowing, we don't stop there. We offer rental options for multiple fashion categories, ensuring that our customers have access to a wide range of items for any occasion.
