How to Rent a Dress with Rent Hives?

Here are Simple Steps!

Sign Up or Log In

New Users: Create an account on our website or app.

Returning Users: Log in to your existing account.

Browse and Select

Explore: Browse our collection by categories like Wedding, Party, etc.

Filters: Use size, color, and price filters to find the perfect dress.

Details: Click on a dress for more info and to check availability.

Virtual Try-On (Optional)

AR Feature: Virtually try on the dress to see how it looks on you.

Add to Cart

Rental Period: Choose how long you want to rent (e.g., 3 days, 7 days).

Accessories: Add matching jewelry, bags, or shoes if needed.

Checkout

Review Order: Check your order summary and apply any discount codes.

Delivery Info: Enter your address and choose a delivery option.

Payment: Complete your payment using our secure gateway.

Enjoy and Return

Wear: Enjoy your rented dress for your event.

Return: Pack the dress in the return packaging and schedule a pickup or drop it off as instructed. We handle the cleaning.

Feedback

Rate & Review: Share your experience to help us improve. With Rent Hives, renting stylish outfits is simple, affordable, and eco-friendly. Enjoy your perfect outfit without the splurge!
